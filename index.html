Our Story

More Than a Church,
We Are a Family.

Founded on the principles of radical grace and unwavering prayer, GOGAP began as a small gathering of believers hungry for a deeper encounter with God. Today, we are a thriving sanctuary where every soul is treated as a precious seed in God's garden.

We believe that through the finished work of Christ, every person has access to a life of victory, peace, and divine purpose. Whether you are searching for answers or looking for a place to call home, our gates are always open.
