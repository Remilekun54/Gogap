Our Story & Vision

The Garden of Grace and Prayer (GOGAP) was birthed out of a divine burden to see believers live a life of victory through the dual pillars of deep spiritual intimacy and practical biblical knowledge.

Our Mission

To cultivate a generation of believers who are spiritually fertile and doctrinally sound.

Our Vision

To see the fragrance of God's grace spread across nations through consistent prayer.
